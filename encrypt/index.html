Create a Private Channel

  1. Open Meshtastic app
  2. Go to Settings â†’ Channels
  3. Tap "+ Add Channel"
  4. Enter a name (e.g., "Alex" or "Family")
  5. Tap PSK â†’ Select "Random" (generates 256-bit key)
  6. Tap Save
  7. Device will reboot

Share With Your Contact

  1. Go to Settings â†’ Channels
  2. Tap your new channel
  3. Tap "Share" or the QR icon
  4. Have your contact scan the QR code
  5. They choose "Add Channel" (not replace!)

Now only you two can read messages on that channel.

ğŸ¤” How does the encryption work? â–¼

When you create a channel with a "Random" PSK (Pre-Shared Key), the app generates a 256-bit AES encryption key. This is the same encryption standard used by banks and governments.

Messages are encrypted on your phone before being transmitted. Even the devices relaying your message can't read it â€” they just pass along the encrypted blob.

Only devices with the exact same key can decrypt and read the messages.

ğŸ’¡ Channel limits â–¼

Meshtastic devices can have up to 8 channels (0-7). Channel 0 is always the primary/public channel.

That means you can have up to 7 private conversations active at once. If you need more, you can delete old channels and create new ones.
